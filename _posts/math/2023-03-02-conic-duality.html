Proposition (Conic duality) $$ \begin{array}[t]{ll} \min\ & c^Tx \\ \textrm{s.t. } & Ax - b \in K \\ & x\in \mathbb R^n \end{array} = \begin{array}[t]{ll} \max\ & b^T\lambda \\ \textrm{s.t. } & A^T\lambda = c \\ & \lambda\in K^* \end{array} $$
