About idol

Idol is a powerful and flexible library meticulously crafted for developing new mathematical optimization algorithms. It is built to provide researchers with a versatile toolkit to construct, tweak, and experiment with state-of-the-art methods. Whether you're exploring Branch-and-Price, Benders decomposition, Column-and-Constraint generation for adjustable robust problems, or any other cutting-edge method, idol is your trusted companion.

I am the creator and developer of idol.
